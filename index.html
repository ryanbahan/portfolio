What's News?

React, News API, Mapquest API

What's new is a simple & efficient news application built in React. It fetches recent news stories via the News API, and serves up news in entertainment, health, technology, and science.

Users can also search by topic, or view location-based news, made possible via the reverse geocoding functionality of Mapquest API.
